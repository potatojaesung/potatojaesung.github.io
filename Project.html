3. Midterm: AI Theory & N-gram Analysis

Course: Computational English Linguistics (Prof. Ahn)
Topic: Generative AI Concepts & Text Generation
Tools: R (Packages: ngram, tidyverse, gutenbergr)

This midterm project covers both the theoretical foundations of AI and practical text analysis. I explored key concepts such as Transformers, GPUs, and the Markov assumption.

Key Activities:

  • Analyzed Lewis Carroll's Alice's Adventures in Wonderland using R.
  • Generated sentences using various N-gram models (Bigram to 30-gram).
  • Compared the trade-off between grammatical accuracy and creativity based on the value of N.
